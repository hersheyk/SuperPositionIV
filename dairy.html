Milk is an essential part of Americans' lives. It is vital to choose the right type of milk. According to this BBC graph of the enviornmental imapct of different type of milks, it is clear that dairy milk is detrimental to the earth due to its large carbon emissions, land use, and water use. However, oat milk and soy milk use the least land and water. They also produce significantly less carbon emissions. Therefore, if you want to consume milk ethically, you should choose vegan alternatives.
